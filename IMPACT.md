# Use Case & Impact

## The Problem
Global enterprises are facing a "Carbon Visibility Gap." With rising carbon taxes (projected to reach $100+/ton in many jurisdictions by 2030), companies lack the real-time intelligence to make operational changes that save both carbon and capital. Existing solutions are static, backward-looking, and manual.

## The Solution: Aura
Aura provides **Autonomous Intelligence**. It doesn't just report on the past; it optimizes the future. By automating data ingestion and providing a "Climate Copilot," Aura turns carbon management into a real-time financial advantage.

## Real-World Use Case
### Scenario: Logistics Optimization
A manufacturing company operates a high-frequency logistics corridor between Berlin and Poznan. 
- **Without Aura:** The company uses trucks, paying standard carbon taxes and missing the high-emission footprint.
- **With Aura:** The Climate Copilot identifies a 3.4t CO2 saving by switching to rail. This shift qualifies the company for a $1,200 tax rebate and improves their overall ESG compliance score by 7%.

## Potential Impact
1.  **Financial:** Reducing carbon tax liabilities by up to 15% through proactive logistics and vendor shifts.
2.  **Operational:** Saving thousands of man-hours per year by automating document ingestion (Auto-Reader).
3.  **Environmental:** Accelerating the transition to net-zero by making "Green" the more profitable operational choice.
