# Aura: The Inspiration 💡

## The "Visibility Gap"
We noticed that most companies *want* to be green, but they don't know *how*. Carbon accounting is currently a manual, backward-looking process. By the time a company gets its emission reports, it's too late to change anything.

## Our Vision
We wanted to build an **Autonomous Carbon Intelligence** that works in real-time. 

### Built With:
- **Google Gemini 1.5 Flash:** The engine that turns raw, unstructured logistics data into strategic insights. We chose Flash for its speed and massive context window (1M+ tokens), allowing us to analyze entire supply chains in seconds.
- **Next.js 15:** For a premium, ultra-fast web experience.
- **Firebase App Hosting:** To ensure global scalability and secure AI key management.

## Why it Matters
Aura turns "Sustainability" from a cost center into a **Profit Engine**. By identifying tax rebates and cheaper green logistics routes, we prove that the greenest path is also the most profitable one.
